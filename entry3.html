Movies About Dreams

These are some of the movies I've found that relate to dreams. I've seen a few of them, and they're highly recommended.

Inception

Inception explores the idea of dream-sharing and how our subconscious shapes reality. The movie delves into the layers of dreams within dreams, showcasing how powerful and dangerous the dream world can be.

Spirited Away

Spirited Away is like a dream brought to life, filled with surreal landscapes and unforgettable characters. It portrays a young girl's journey through a mysterious spirit world, where reality blurs and lessons of courage and growth are learned.

The Science of Sleep

The Science of Sleep blurs the line between dreams and reality. Through its quirky visuals and imaginative sequences, it reflects on how our dreams influence emotions and relationships.
