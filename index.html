Dream World

Entry 1

Standard Dream

The standard dream is the most common kind of dream, in which the environment is very ordinary and the characters are familiar. These dreams happen at different times and places.

Entry 2

Daydreams

Daydreaming is a kind of fantasy in which people's wishes or other desired things are realized.

Entry 3

False Awakening Dreams

False awakening dreams are when people feel they have gotten up in the morning to do something, but find themselves dreaming instead. This is also known as false waking.
